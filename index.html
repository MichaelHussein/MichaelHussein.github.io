Bike Sales Analysis (Excel)

In the Bike Sales Analysis project, I explored a dataset of 1,000 customer records to uncover factors influencing bike purchases. Using Excel, I applied pivot tables, filters, and statistical functions to analyze demographics, income, and commute patterns. Key insights revealed higher purchase rates among professionals and shorter commuters, guiding targeted marketing strategies

SQL Murder Mystery

Shop Trends Analysis (Python)

In the Shopping Trends Analysis project, I used Python and Pandas to analyze customer purchase data, revealing insights like a $40,777 total for free shipping, a $59.28 average for discount users, and clothing as the top category for female customers with low ratings. Analysis by location, payment, and color informed marketing strategies.

Adventure Works (Power BI)

In the Adventure Works project, I utilized Power BI and DAX queries to calculate key performance indicators, including revenue, profit, COGS, and total customers, from the Adventure Works dataset. Visualizations of revenue and profit by country and revenue by month provided actionable insights to support stakeholder decision-making.
